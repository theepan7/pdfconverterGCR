How to Split a PDF Online

  1. Click the upload area above and select your PDF file.
  2. Enter the start page and end page numbers you want to extract.
  3. Click Split PDF — your extracted pages will download as a new PDF.

When to Split a PDF

Splitting a PDF is handy when you need to share only part of a document, extract a chapter from a book, or separate invoices bundled into a single file.
