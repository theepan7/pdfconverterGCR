    1. Drop your images onto the upload area above, or click to browse.

    4. Click Convert to PDF — your PDF downloads automatically.

    Why Convert Images to PDF?

    PDF files are universally supported, preserve image quality, and are easier to share as a single file. Converting photos, scans, or screenshots to PDF is ideal for submitting documents, archiving records, or sharing presentations.
