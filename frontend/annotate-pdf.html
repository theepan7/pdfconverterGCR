How to Annotate a PDF Online

  1. Upload your PDF by clicking the drop zone or dragging a file onto it.
  2. Choose an annotation tool from the toolbar: Highlight, Underline, Strikethrough, or Comment.
  3. Click and drag on the page to place your annotation. For comments, a text box will appear.
  4. Navigate between pages using the arrow buttons and annotate as many pages as needed.
  5. Click Save Annotated PDF — your file downloads instantly with all annotations baked in.

About PDF Annotation

Highlights, underlines, and strikethroughs are drawn directly onto each page as coloured shapes. Comments are embedded as sticky notes visible in any PDF reader. No data ever leaves your device.
