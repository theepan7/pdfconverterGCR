How to Delete Pages from a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF by clicking the drop zone or dragging a file onto it.
  2. Type the page numbers or ranges you want to remove and press Enter or , to add each one (e.g. 3, 5-8, 12).
  3. Check the preview — it shows how many pages will be deleted and how many kept.
  4. Click Delete Pages — your cleaned PDF downloads automatically.

When to Delete PDF Pages

Removing pages is useful when you want to strip a cover page before sharing, remove blank or duplicate pages from a scan, or trim a long report down to only the relevant sections.
