    How to Rotate PDF Pages Online

    1. Drop your PDF onto the upload area, or click to browse and select a file.
    2. Each page is displayed as a thumbnail. Click the rotate buttons beneath any page to rotate it 90° left or right.
    3. Use Select All and the bulk rotation chips to rotate many pages at once.
    4. Click Rotate & Download — your modified PDF downloads immediately.

    When to Rotate PDF Pages

    Scanned documents often have pages in the wrong orientation. Use this tool to fix landscape pages that should be portrait, correct upside-down scans, or rotate a single page in a multi-page report — all without installing any software.
