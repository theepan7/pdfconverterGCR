    How to Merge PDFs Online

    1. Drop your PDF files onto the upload area, or click to browse.
    2. Click Add more files to pick PDFs stored in other folders.
    3. Drag the rows up or down to set the exact merge order you want.
    4. Click Merge PDFs — your combined file downloads automatically.

    When to Merge PDF Files

    Merging PDFs is useful when you want to send multiple documents as a single attachment, combine chapters of a report, or consolidate invoices into one file.
