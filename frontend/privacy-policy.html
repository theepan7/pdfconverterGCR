About Privacy

Privacy Policy

We built Mini PDF Tools with privacy at the core — not as an afterthought.

Home › Privacy Policy
Last updated: 1 January 2025 Effective immediately upon publication

Your files are never stored

Files you upload are processed in server memory and discarded immediately after your download completes. We have no copy of your documents.

No account required — no personal data collected

We don't ask for your name, email, or any personal information to use any tool on this site.

We never sell your data

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any information about you or your files with third parties for commercial purposes.

1. Who we are

Mini PDF Tools (minipdftools.com) is a free online PDF toolkit. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding that information. By using our website and tools, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@minipdftools.com.

2. Files and documents you upload

When you use a tool on Mini PDF Tools, you upload a file to our processing server. Here is exactly what happens to that file:

  • The file is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our API server hosted on Google Cloud Run.
  • The file is loaded into server memory for processing (compression, merging, splitting, etc.).
  • The processed result is sent back to your browser as a download.
  • The original file and the processed result are immediately discarded from memory. Nothing is written to disk, a database, or cloud storage.

We never read, analyse, index, or retain the contents of your files. We have no ability to access your documents after the processing request completes.

3. Information we collect automatically

Like most websites, we collect certain non-personal technical information automatically when you visit our site. This includes:

Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — which pages are visited, how long sessions last, and what tools are most used. Google Analytics collects anonymised data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country or city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

This data is aggregated and anonymised. We cannot identify individual visitors from this data. Google Analytics data is processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Server logs

Our API server (Google Cloud Run) generates structured logs for each processing request. These logs record:

  • The operation performed (compress, merge, split, etc.)
  • File size in kilobytes (not the file itself)
  • Processing time in milliseconds
  • Success or error status

Logs do not contain file contents, file names, or any information that could identify you personally. Logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging and service monitoring purposes, after which they are automatically deleted.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity across websites. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Google's advertising policies.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using the NAI opt-out tool.

4. Cookies

We do not set first-party cookies ourselves. However, third-party services we use (Google Analytics and Google AdSense) may set cookies in your browser. These cookies help Google measure site traffic and deliver relevant advertisements.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of third-party services on our site.

5. How we use information

The limited, anonymised data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how our tools are being used so we can improve them
  • Monitor server performance and diagnose technical issues
  • Detect and prevent abuse or excessive use that could degrade service for others
  • Display relevant advertisements to keep the service free

We do not use any collected data for marketing, profiling, or selling to third parties.

6. Third-party services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate Mini PDF Tools:

  • Google Cloud Run — hosts our PDF processing API. Files are transmitted to and processed within Google's infrastructure. Google Cloud's data processing terms apply.
  • Cloudflare Pages — hosts our website files and provides CDN delivery. Cloudflare may process IP addresses to route traffic and provide security.
  • Google Analytics — provides anonymised usage analytics.
  • Google AdSense — serves advertisements on our pages.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns about how they handle data.

7. Data security

All data transmitted between your browser and our services is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS). Our processing API does not persist any file data. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect against unauthorised access to any data we do process.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we do everything reasonably possible to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's privacy

Mini PDF Tools is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

9. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data and do not store your files, there is very little data for you to request, correct, or delete. However, if you believe we hold any information about you, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we may hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@minipdftools.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Mini PDF Tools after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users via a notice on our homepage.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@minipdftools.com
  • Website: minipdftools.com

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 48 hours on business days.
