Our mission

Mini PDF Tools was created with one goal: give everyone access to fast, capable PDF tools without friction. No account to create. No subscription to manage. No watermarks on your files. Just open a page, drop your file, and get your work done.

Whether you're a student compressing a thesis, a small business merging invoices, or someone rotating a scanned document on their phone — every tool on this site is completely free, and we intend to keep it that way.

What we stand for

Private by design

Your files are processed in memory and immediately discarded. We never write your documents to disk, never log their contents, and never share them with third parties.

Fast cloud processing

Powered by Google Cloud for consistent speed on any device or connection. Pages are optimised to load instantly — no bloat, no waiting.

No limits, no tricks

No daily limits, no hidden file-size caps that force an upgrade, and no watermarks on your output. Every feature is genuinely free — no bait-and-switch.

Works in any browser

No software to download. Mini PDF Tools runs entirely in your browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, and mobile browsers are all fully supported.

Simplicity first

Each tool does exactly one thing well. Clean interface, no manual needed. If you can drag and drop a file, you can use Mini PDF Tools.

Community supported

Kept free through unobtrusive advertising. We never let ads interfere with the tool experience, and we never sell user data to advertisers.
