    How to Split a PDF into Multiple Ranges

    1. Upload your PDF by clicking the drop zone or dragging a file onto it.
    2. Click Add range and enter a Start and End page (e.g. 1–10).
    3. Keep adding more ranges — 20–30, 50–60, or any pages you need.
    4. Drag the range rows to reorder them if needed.
    5. Click Split PDF — each range downloads as its own file.

    When to Split a PDF

    Splitting is ideal for extracting chapters from a long report, sharing only relevant pages with a client, or breaking a 100-page document into smaller, focused sections.
